Our Faculties

Three Faculties, One Mission

St. Mary's University College offers bachelor's degree programs through three specialized faculties

Faculty

Faculty of Rehabilitation Sciences

Our founding faculty, established in 2008, prepares healthcare professionals specialized in rehabilitation services. With an estimated 50,000 persons with disabilities in South Sudan, our graduates play a critical role in providing essential care and support to vulnerable populations.

The program is delivered in partnership with OVCI la Nostra Famiglia, which operates the Usratuna Rehabilitation Center adjacent to our campus. Students benefit from hands-on clinical experience in physiotherapy, occupational therapy, and speech therapy.

Program Offered

Bachelor of Science in Rehabilitation

Duration: 4 Years

Focus Areas:

  • Physiotherapy
  • Occupational Therapy
  • Speech Therapy
  • Community-Based Rehabilitation
  • Disability Studies
Faculty

Faculty of Education

The Faculty of Education trains qualified teachers and educators to transform South Sudan's educational landscape. Recognizing that quality education is fundamental to national development, we prepare graduates who can serve in primary, secondary, and community education settings.

Our program combines theoretical foundations in pedagogy with practical teaching experience. Students learn modern teaching methodologies while understanding the unique context of education in South Sudan.

Program Offered

Bachelor of Education

Duration: 4 Years

Focus Areas:

  • Primary Education
  • Secondary Education
  • Educational Psychology
  • Curriculum Development
  • Inclusive Education
Faculty

Faculty of Human Development

Established in 2019, the Faculty of Human Development is our newest addition, offering programs that address South Sudan's critical need for trained social workers, community development specialists, and public administrators.

This faculty provides the first programs of their kind in Juba, preparing graduates who can contribute to conflict resolution, peace-building, and cooperative construction of a better South Sudan.

Programs Offered

Bachelor of Social Work and Social Administration

Duration: 4 Years

Training professional social workers to serve vulnerable populations, families, and communities across South Sudan.

Bachelor of Human Development

Duration: 4 Years

Preparing specialists in community development, capacity building, and human resource development.

Bachelor of Public Administration

Duration: 4 Years

Training future leaders for government service and public sector management.

Academic Facilities

Supporting your learning journey with essential resources

University Library

Our library provides students with access to academic books, journals, and research materials essential for their studies. The library serves as a quiet space for individual study and group discussions.

Clinical Training Facilities

Students in Rehabilitation Sciences gain practical experience at the adjacent Usratuna Rehabilitation Center, which offers physiotherapy, speech therapy, and occupational therapy services.

Teaching Practice

Education students complete supervised teaching practice at local schools, gaining hands-on classroom experience under the guidance of experienced educators.
