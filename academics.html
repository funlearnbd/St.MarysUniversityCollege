Academic Programs

Three faculties offering programs designed to meet South Sudan's professional needs

Founded 2008

Faculty of Rehabilitation Sciences

Our founding faculty, established in partnership with Usratuna Rehabilitation Center, addresses the critical need for trained rehabilitation professionals in South Sudan.

Bachelor of Science in Rehabilitation

Duration: 4 Years

This comprehensive program trains students to provide rehabilitation services to individuals with disabilities and those recovering from illness or injury.

Focus Areas

  • Physiotherapy
  • Occupational Therapy
  • Speech and Language Therapy
  • Community-Based Rehabilitation
  • Disability Studies
  • Assistive Technology

Clinical Training Partnership

Students receive hands-on clinical training at Usratuna Rehabilitation Center, gaining practical experience working with patients under the supervision of experienced professionals. This partnership ensures graduates are job-ready upon completion of their studies.

Teacher Training

Faculty of Education

Preparing dedicated teachers for South Sudan's schools with comprehensive training in pedagogy, subject matter expertise, and inclusive education practices.

Bachelor of Education

Duration: 4 Years

A comprehensive program preparing students for careers in primary and secondary education.

Focus Areas

  • Primary Education Methods
  • Secondary Education Methods
  • Educational Psychology
  • Curriculum Development
  • Inclusive Education
  • Educational Administration

Teaching Practice

Education students complete supervised teaching practice in local schools, applying their learning in real classroom environments. This practical experience is essential for developing effective teaching skills.

Established 2019

Faculty of Human Development

Our newest faculty, offering programs that address South Sudan's growing need for community development and public administration professionals.

Bachelor of Social Work & Social Administration

Duration: 4 Years

Training professionals to address social issues, support vulnerable populations, and administer social services programs.

Bachelor of Human Development

Duration: 4 Years

A broad program focusing on human growth, community development, and strategies for improving quality of life.

Bachelor of Public Administration

Duration: 4 Years

Preparing future leaders for careers in government, NGOs, and public sector organizations.
