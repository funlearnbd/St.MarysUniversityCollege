Campus Visits

We welcome prospective students and their families to visit our campus. A campus visit is a wonderful opportunity to:

  • Tour our facilities and classrooms
  • Meet with faculty and current students
  • Learn about our academic programs
  • Discuss admission requirements
  • Experience our campus community

To schedule a campus visit, please contact the Admissions Office at least one week in advance.
