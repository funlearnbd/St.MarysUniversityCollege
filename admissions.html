Admissions

Begin your journey at St. Mary's University College

Welcome

Your Future Starts Here

St. Mary's University College welcomes applications from all eligible citizens of South Sudan who are committed to serving their communities through education, healthcare, and social development.

As a Catholic institution, we seek students who demonstrate academic potential, personal integrity, and a desire to contribute to the rebuilding of South Sudan. Our admission process is designed to identify students who will thrive in our supportive learning environment.

We encourage prospective students to visit our campus, meet with faculty members, and learn more about our programs before applying.

Requirements

Admission Criteria

What you need to apply for undergraduate programs

Academic Requirements

  • South Sudan Certificate of Secondary Education (SSCSE) or equivalent
  • Minimum grade requirements as specified by each faculty
  • Proficiency in English (language of instruction)
  • Strong foundation in relevant subjects for chosen program

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Original secondary school certificate and transcripts
  • National ID or valid passport
  • Passport-sized photographs (4)
  • Letter of recommendation from school or community leader
  • Medical fitness certificate

Personal Qualities

  • Commitment to serving South Sudan
  • Good moral character
  • Willingness to abide by university regulations
  • Dedication to academic excellence
  • Respect for Catholic values and traditions
How to Apply

Application Process

Follow these steps to submit your application

Step 1: Obtain Application Form

Application forms are available at the University Admissions Office on our campus in Juba. You may also contact us to request a form by email.

Step 2: Complete the Form

Fill out the application form completely and accurately. Incomplete applications will not be processed. Ensure all required documents are attached.

Step 3: Pay Application Fee

Submit the non-refundable application fee at the university Finance Office. Keep the receipt as proof of payment.

Step 4: Submit Application

Submit your completed application with all supporting documents and payment receipt to the Admissions Office before the deadline.

Step 5: Await Decision

Applications are reviewed by the Admissions Committee. Successful applicants will be notified by letter and invited for enrollment.

Costs

Tuition & Fees

Information about educational costs

Tuition Fees

Tuition fees vary by program. As a private institution supported by the Catholic Church and OVCI, we strive to keep fees affordable while maintaining quality education.

Please contact the Finance Office for current tuition rates and payment schedules for your program of interest.

Additional Costs

  • Application fee (non-refundable)
  • Registration fee
  • Student activity fee
  • Library fee
  • Examination fee
  • Accommodation (if applicable)

Financial Assistance

Limited scholarships and financial assistance may be available for qualified students demonstrating financial need. Contact the Student Affairs Office for information about available support.

Student Life

Life at St. Mary's

Accommodation

The university campus provides housing facilities for students. On-campus accommodation helps create a supportive community environment for learning and personal growth.

Spiritual Life

As a Catholic institution, we offer opportunities for spiritual growth through Mass, prayer services, and faith formation programs. Students of all faiths are welcome and respected.

Student Support

Our Student Affairs Office provides academic advising, personal counseling, and support services to help students succeed in their studies and personal development.
