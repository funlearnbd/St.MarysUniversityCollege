About Us

Discover our history, mission, and commitment to Catholic higher education in South Sudan

Our History

A Legacy of Service

St. Mary's University College was founded in 2008 and officially inaugurated on April 15, 2009. The institution emerged from a collaborative vision shared by three founding partners committed to advancing higher education in South Sudan.

Our founding Faculty of Rehabilitation Sciences was established in partnership with Usratuna Rehabilitation Center, addressing the critical need for trained rehabilitation professionals in a nation affected by decades of conflict.

Since then, we have expanded to include the Faculty of Education and, in 2019, the Faculty of Human Development, continuously growing to meet South Sudan's evolving professional needs.

Our Foundation

Founding Partners

Three organizations united by a shared commitment to education and service

Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Juba

The Archdiocese provides spiritual guidance and institutional oversight, ensuring our education remains grounded in Catholic values and tradition.

OVCI la Nostra Famiglia

An Italian Catholic NGO that has served South Sudan since 1983, providing technical expertise and international partnerships, particularly in rehabilitation sciences.

Government of South Sudan

Through the Ministry of Gender, Social Welfare and Religious Affairs, the government supports our mission to train professionals for national development.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide quality Catholic higher education that forms competent, compassionate professionals committed to serving the people of South Sudan, particularly those most in need.

Vision: To be a leading institution of higher learning in South Sudan, recognized for academic excellence, ethical leadership, and service to the community.

Core Values

  • Faith and spiritual formation
  • Academic excellence and integrity
  • Service to the community
  • Respect for human dignity
  • Commitment to social justice
  • Collaboration and partnership
Recognition

Accreditation & Affiliation

Government Recognition

St. Mary's University College is officially recognized by the Ministry of Education, Science and Technology of the Republic of South Sudan. Our programs meet national standards for higher education and our degrees are recognized throughout South Sudan.

Catholic Affiliation

As a ministry of the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Juba, we operate under the guidance of the Catholic Church and uphold the principles of Catholic education. Our institution is part of the global network of Catholic universities and colleges.

Leadership

University Administration

Office of the Vice Chancellor

The Vice Chancellor provides academic and administrative leadership for the university, ensuring that our programs maintain the highest standards of quality and that our institution fulfills its mission of service to South Sudan.

In Memoriam: We honor the memory of our founding Vice Chancellor, the late Archbishop Paulino Lukudu Loro (1940-2021), whose vision and dedication made St. Mary's University College a reality.
