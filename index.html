Our Programs

Academic Faculties

Three distinguished faculties offering programs designed to meet South Sudan's critical professional needs.

Rehabilitation Sciences

Our founding faculty, established in 2008 in partnership with Usratuna Rehabilitation Center, training specialists in physiotherapy, occupational therapy, and community-based rehabilitation.

Learn More

Education

Preparing dedicated teachers for South Sudan's schools with comprehensive training in primary and secondary education, educational psychology, and inclusive education practices.

Learn More

Human Development

Established in 2019, offering programs in Social Work, Human Development, and Public Administration to address South Sudan's growing need for community development professionals.
