Welcome

Building Tomorrow's Leaders

St. Mary's University College was officially launched on April 15, 2009 by Late H.G. Paulino Lukudu Loro, Metropolitan Archbishop of the Catholic Archdiocese of Juba. The institution began as a collaboration between the Archdiocese and OVCI (Usratuna), an Italian organization dedicated to rehabilitation services.

Today, we comprise four schools offering degree programs in Rehabilitation Sciences, Education, Human Development, and Economics & Administrative Sciences. We are proud to have witnessed eight successful graduations, producing professionals who serve South Sudan across various sectors.
