Welcome to SMUJ

Education for Service

Founded in 2008 through a collaborative effort between the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Juba, the Ministry of Gender, Social Welfare and Religious Affairs, and OVCI la Nostra Famiglia, St. Mary's University College stands as a beacon of hope for South Sudan's youth.

Located in the heart of Juba, we are committed to training skilled professionals who will lead the nation's development in healthcare, education, and social services.
